What is FRZN?

FRZN is a code redemption system alike to platforms like hzm.gg. Users, particularly those with premium access, have the ability to generate unique codes through FRZN. These codes can be redeemed in-game, on Discord, or via the website, resulting in the direct transfer of Robux to the user's account. The system offers a convenient and straightforward way for individuals to access and benefit from their Robux rewards.
